LIL HEARTS DAYCARE'S PROGRAMS
‘Educators with Playful Hearts and Teaching Minds.’

There are three primary educational programs offered at Lil Hearts Daycare, as are found at most certified facilities - they are:

  • 1) Infant   2) Toddler   and   3) Pre-School
And like many of top-rated daycares, as per the guidelines of Ministry of Education and other recognized scholastic and teaching resources, LHD utilizes all of the foundational principles of learning within their programs. Some of these numerous key tenants include:

  • A) How Does Learning Happen - The 4 Foundations (Belonging, Well-Being, Engagement &Expression).
  • B) Domain & Skills - Cognitive, Communication, Emotional, Literacy, Physical, & Social.
  • C) ELECT - Early Learning for Every Child Today.
  • D) Continuum of Development.
  • E) Play Days.
