OUR OFFER 
‘ Ensuring a Great Start to Your Child’s Future. ’

The first 4 to 5 years of your child’s life is the most important in so many ways. Speech, small and large motor functions, social adaptation, mental and emotional stability - and the list goes on.

That means your daycare choice is critical. So to help you make the best decision for you and your baby boy and / or baby girl (or both) - Lil Hearts Daycare has created our simple yet comprehensive tool known as ‘SAFE Daycare ’ - an exclusive LHD methodology and process that allows you, the parent - to make the best and right decision for your child. Because at Lil Hearts, we have not forgotten, as some have, what is most important - the heart of the child. It’s not who has the greatest number of kids, the longest list of books and programs, or the most write-ups in magazines or on social media - it’s about the kids. [Read More...]

 

OUR MISSION 
‘ To Teach Children the Joy of Learning. ’

Every dream for a better tomorrow has to have a basis of reality, a place to start. For the most critical part in laying the foundation for success in school and therefore later in life - is joy. Think about it, how many things have we consistently continued to engage in that we didn’t enjoy? The list is endless! If we were not enJOYing the experience - the effort was short lived, because we were unhappy. So it’s amazing to witness the growing number of daycare programs that more resemble a Masters Degree course in university than a pre-school for little kids. Truly foolish. Lil Hearts does not only not recommend such a ridiculous beginning to your child’s education - but we emphatically warn against it! For there are few required foundations in the overall development of your child that are as critical as the love and ‘joy of learning’. Kids are just that - kids. They love to play and have fun, and the more they enJOY learning, the more the will want to learn more and more - for the rest of their lives! [Read More...]

 

OUR VISION 
‘ To Change the World Through Love and Education. ’

Today’s society is not the life of days gone by with kids walking merrily off to school, with Mom at home baking waiting for Dad to come home - the whole family then sitting happily around the dinner table with not a care in world. Today everything moves at the speed of the internet, educational demands are through the roof, and we worry about the safety of children in our increasingly violent and dangerous cities. And as a result, many more children are not able to cope with the increased pressure placed on them by the curriculums and even some parents - to excel in this scholastic pressure cooker. However, we at Lil Hearts Daycare believe that we can help change this trend, and safeguard our little ones - allowing them to enjoy a more satisfying and successful educational journey. How? By filling them with the belief and knowledge, that love and kindness and a caring heart, combined with the mental and emotional satisfaction of learning - results in approval and an increased sense of self-worth and confidence.
